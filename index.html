Favorite Holiday Destination : SHIMLA


Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh, situated at an elevation of 2200 meters (7200 feet) above sea level. Once the summer capital of British India, Shimla still retains its old-world charm with beautiful colonial architecture. The pedestrian-friendly Mall Road and the ridge lined with multiple shops, cafes, and restaurants are delightful to explore. Shimla is well-connected by road and is approximately 4 hours away from the nearby city of Chandigarh. It also has an airport, although there arenâ€™t many daily flights. The famous Kalka-Shimla train route is a UNESCO-listed World Heritage site, offering scenic views during the journey.
